# ❓ Q&A Prompts

## Prompt 1: Contextual Q&A
Answer the question based on the provided context.  
If the answer is not in the context, say "Not found."  
**Context:** "The capability approach emphasizes real freedoms and functionings."  
**Question:** What is the main benefit of the capability approach?

---

## Prompt 2: Reasoning Q&A (CoT)
**Question:** Tracy used a wire 4 feet long, cut into 6-inch pieces. How many pieces?  
**Answer:** Let's think step by step.

---

## Prompt 3: Multi-Expert Q&A (ToT)
Imagine three experts are answering this question.  
Each expert writes one step of their reasoning, then compares with the group.  
If any expert realizes they are wrong, they leave.  
**Question:** How many passengers remain if 48 are on a bus and 8 get off at the first stop?
